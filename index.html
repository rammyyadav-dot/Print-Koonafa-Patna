What is the minimum order for visiting cards?+
Minimum order is 100 visiting cards starting at ₹199. For velvet, spot UV, or gold foil, minimum is 200 cards. We recommend 500 cards (₹819) as the best value for professionals.
Do you offer same-day printing in Patna?+
Yes! Orders placed before 2 PM IST are dispatched same evening. Pickup from Raja Bazar studio from 6 PM. Eligible: visiting cards, A4 stationery, labels, small banners, stickers.
What file formats do you accept for printing?+
We accept PDF (preferred), AI, PSD, CDR, and high-res PNG/JPEG (300 DPI min). Include 3mm bleed. If you don't have a design, we offer free design for orders above ₹2,000.
Can I get a GST invoice for my order?+
Yes. Koonafa Print Studio is GST-registered. All orders above ₹500 include a GST invoice. Provide your GSTIN at order time for proper B2B billing. GST: 12% on print, 5% on packaging.
Do you deliver outside Patna?+
Yes. Bihar delivery (Gaya, Muzaffarpur, Bhagalpur) via local courier in 1–3 days. Pan-India via DTDC/Delhivery in 3–7 days. Charges calculated at checkout.
What is your reprint and refund policy?+
100% free reprint if the error is ours (colour mismatch, wrong cut, defect). Raise a complaint within 48 hours with photos. Reprints processed within 24 hours. Refunds for cancellations within 1 hour of placement.
